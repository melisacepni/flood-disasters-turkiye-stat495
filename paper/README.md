## Overview

## Methods
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Regression Analysis
- Chi-Square Goodness-of-Fit Tests
- ANOVA

## Data Source
- EM-DAT (Emergency Events Database)

## Key Findings
- No significant long-term trend in flood frequency
- Floods are most frequent during summer
- Black Sea Region is the most affected area
- Economic losses exceed 2 billion USD over 25 years
